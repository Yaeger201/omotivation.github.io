People are born to work and strive for improvement. Motivation is one of the main things that boost people to exceed their limits, and improve. Being able to understand motivation would grant us a valuable perception of human nature. It shall elucidate why we set goals and strive for improvement.

Simple tricks to motivate yourself.

1. Create a positive Environment.

Songs may have the biggest impact in that part. As an example you can listen to “Time” by Hans Zimmer, it's one of the best.

2. Celebrate your small wins.

Always remember the road to our big goals is accomplished through small steps, so remember to celebrate each one of them and enjoy it. That will keep your motivation always up as you will feel the progress you are making.

3. Surround Yourself With Motivated People.

Positive environment will have a big impact on you and how your life improves. American entrepreneur John Rohn once said, “You’re the average of the five people you spend the most time with.”
